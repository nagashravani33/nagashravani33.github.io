Mahatma Gandhi

Biography

Early life and education

Mohandas karamchand Gandhi,also known as Mahathma Gandhi, was born on October 2,1869,in Porbandar, a costal town present-day Gujarat, India .He was born into a Hindu Merchant caste family and the youngest of four childern

Education

Gandhi was a good student and completed his early education in India.He then went to Londan to study law and was called to the bar in 1891.He returned to India and set up a law practice in Bombay,but he was not successful

The Fight for Indian Independence

Gandhi become involved in the Indian independence movement in 1915,after he was asked to lead the Indian National Congress.He become known for hiss philosophy of nonviolent resitance, which he called Satyagraha. Gandhi les sevaral nonviolentcampaigns and protests againest British rule,including the Salt March in1930 and the Quit India movement in 1942

Imprisonment

Gandhi was aressted several times for is involment in the independence movement and spent a total of about seven years in prison.While in prison, he continued to write and advocate for nonviolent resistance
