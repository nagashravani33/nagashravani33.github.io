What is Artifical Intelligence

Artifical Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by computer systems. These processes include learning (the acquisition of information and rules for using the information) ,reasoning (using the rule to reach appoximate or defenite conclusions),and self-corrections include expert systems,speech recognition, and machine vision
